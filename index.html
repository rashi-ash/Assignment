Computer Science

The Department has been functioning since the inception of the college in 1995. One of the largest computer science departments under the University of Calicut, the PG Course was started in 2001, with10 seats. The department has a pass percentage of 100 in almost all the years. Two out of the Nine faculty members are Ph.D holders and the rest of the faculty members are pursuing their research. The department has produced quality professionals, holding important positions in Information Technology industry in India and abroad. Our notable alumni members have been excelling in key posts in various reputed organizations. Our students have also been pursuing research in reputed universities and institutions. The department conducts National seminars almost every year inviting eminent resource persons from the educational and industrial fields. The department is recognized as a Research Centre during 2018-19. In order to inculcate research and innovation in the field of computing, a special research room with lab is setup under the Department of Computer Science. Besides academic courses and soft skills training, research and innovations are given due importance. Remedial coaching to the weak students has improved the academic performance of weaker students.

Broadcasting & Journalism

The college has started two B Voc degree programmes during the year 2014-15. Sanctioned by the University Grants Commission (UGC), this newly designed, novel graduate programme - Bachelor of Vocation programme - is a part of Commission’s effort to promote skill development based higher education, incorporating specific job rule under the National Skill Qualification Frame work (NSQF).

Similar to conventional degree programmes, B.Voc follows the pattern of three year credit and semester system. But, unlike other graduate programmes, B.Voc offers multiple entry and exit options. Students who are completing the first year will be getting a diploma, and an advanced diploma after the second year and a degree certificate on completion of the entire three year programme.

The department is offering a three year B.Voc Programme in Broadcasting & Journalism with an intake of 50 students. The College has created linkages and signed MoUs with various media organizations for the skill training of the students.

The department has a full-fledged Department Library and a Lecture Hall with public address system.

OUR CAMPUS

Sullamussalam Science College is a first grade aided college affiliated to University of Calicut, approved by University Grants Commission and accredited by the NAAC with A grade. The College is located on the picturesque hills of Perumparamba on the banks of the river Chaliyar, a kilometer away from Areacode town in Malappuram District. The college is run by Muslim Educational Association, an educational organization formed in 1994 under the auspices of Jamiyyathul Mujahideen Sangam, one of the pioneer reformist muslim educational movements in Malabar. The college was founded with the objective of imparting quality education to backward minority communities in rural areas of Malabar. Thanks to the Almighty, it has so far triumphed in its objectives, despite various constraints.  Now on a stable footing, the college is venturing into new avenues of higher education. Policies and Special features The management believes in Islamic values and in the true spirit of education and thus totally against the commercialization of education. No capitation fee for staff appointments and student admissions Consistently excellent academic records Many prestigious University ranks No politics based elections The campus is free from strikes Exemplary student democratic activities Many clubs and forums meant for all-round development of students.

SS Library

The college library, started 1996, situated in the central part of the campus is one among fully automated notable libraries focused on quality based collection and best services in Kerala. The Library is a member in the N-list Programme under INFLIBNET. Including the Digital Library all functions of the library is developed in the light of S.R Ranganathan`s theory of `Five Laws of Library Science`. All days except sundays and public holidays are working days from 8.30am to 5.00pm.

Digital Library is available in the all Departments of the College, Women Resource Centre, Computer Labs and Language Lab through Campus Network . Separate sections are arranged for the users especially in Reference Service, Career Guidance Corner, Comparative Religion, Bound volumes of the Journals, CD Bank, Question Bank, Subject Books.
